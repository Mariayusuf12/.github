Our Story

Founded in 2015, Sanaag Restaurant brings the authentic flavors of Somalia to Minnesota. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, and we take pride in serving traditional Somali dishes made with the finest ingridents and spices.

Named after the beautiful Sanag Region of Somalia, Our Restaurant aims to provide not just a meal, but an experience that transports you to the vibrant culture and warm hospitality of East Africa.
